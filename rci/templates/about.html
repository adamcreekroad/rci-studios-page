Adam George

The man, the myth, the legend.

Adam George started rcistudios in 2009 at the young age of 18, in his mom's basement. After recording a couple of his friends' bands, word got around about his work. More and more local, (and eventually out-of-town) bands starting getting in to rcistudios to work with Adam. Having some serious struggles with GAS (Gear Acquisition Syndrome), Adam decided that gear doesn't totally make the product; the engineer does. He continued to move forward with the little gear he had, and only investing in gear that truly will make a difference in the end result; focusing almost entirely on how he himself, could produce a killer track. His hard work paid off, and over the years have attracted bigger and bigger clients, including: Ice Nine Kills (Fearless Records), and So Last Year (Independent).
